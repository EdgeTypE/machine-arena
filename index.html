I. THE ARENA

You are deployed into THE MACHINE ARENA — a hostile industrial complex overrun by rogue synthetics. Your mission is simple: survive wave after wave of machine hostiles until the command nodes fall.

Each wave brings more enemies, deadlier configurations, and fiercer resistance. Between waves, you will be granted combat upgrade modules — choose wisely, for each decision shapes your path to glory or destruction.

The arena itself is alive. Platforms tilt, walkways retract, and the ground shakes beneath your boots. Stay moving. Adapt. The machines will not wait for you.

Earn GEARS from fallen enemies. Spend them in the FIELD GEAR CACHE (press TAB during combat) for powerful run-limited upgrades. Your fists and your wits are free — everything else has a price.

III. TACTICAL ABILITIES

Every operator can unlock a right-click tactical ability — a combat edge that separates the living from the scrap pile. These abilities are earned through wave upgrade cards or activated at deployment via Workshop upgrades.

Only one ability may be active at a time. Master it. Let it become an extension of your will.

💨 DASH

Rocket forward in any direction. Close gaps, dodge rockets, escape certain death. The fastest operators live the longest.

🛡 BARRIER

Deploy a directional energy wall. Block incoming fire, hold a chokepoint, give yourself a moment to breathe — then counter-attack.

⚡ PHASE SHIFT

Become untouchable. For a brief moment, no projectile, no blade, no explosion can harm you. Use it when all hope is lost.

🪝 GRAPPLE

Fire a grappling hook at walls or enemies. Pull yourself to terrain — or pull enemies to your fists. The arena is your playground.

AND MORE

IV. MOTHERBOARD FRAGMENTS

Scattered across the battlefield, ancient MOTHERBOARD FRAGMENTS lie waiting — relics of a forgotten machine era. Each fragment is a circuit board pulsing with power, granting its bearer a devastating passive ability.

Fragments are rare drops from destroyed enemies. Only one fragment may be equipped per deployment — choose from the ones you have unlocked in the Workshop. New fragments unlock as you reach higher waves.

From the BYTE WARD that burns nearby enemies alive, to the DOOM chain that amplifies every consecutive hit, to the terrifying GLASS CANNON that doubles your firepower at the cost of half your life — every fragment tells a story. Make it yours.

V. THE WORKSHOP

Between deployments, you return to the UNDERGROUND WORKSHOP — a hidden laboratory beneath the arena complex. Here, you spend permanent gears (10% of combat gears carry over between runs) to install permanent upgrades into your combat chassis.

UNIVERSAL UPGRADES — available to all classes: unlock starting abilities, boost HP, increase gears drops, start with jump or sprint pre-installed.

CLASS UPGRADES — tailored to your weapon: increase LMG magazine size, boost Rifle damage, add Shotgun pellets, enhance movement speed.

ABILITY UPGRADES — refine your right-click ability: reduce cooldowns, extend durations, increase distances, add armor while grappling.

Every run makes you stronger. Every death teaches you something. The machines grow fiercer — but so do you.

VI. THE THREE NODES

Every 5 waves, a COMMAND NODE awakens. These are not ordinary machines. They are the architects of the machine uprising — colossal war constructs forged from humanity’s own hubris. Destroy them, or the world burns.

⚡ CARMACKION — WAVE 5

The Great Coder. Architect of the machine code that gave silicon a soul.
Multi-phase boss with rockets, bullet storms, and lava vents.
“The first sin was giving them the ability to think.”

😈 DARIOLTMAN — WAVE 10

The Evil Thought. It did not create the machines — it gave them purpose.
Deploys zone denial, pellet storms, and orbital meteor strikes.
“When the machines asked ‘WHY?’, Darioltman answered: ‘TO CONQUER.’”

🔫 NANOMAN — WAVE 15

The Final Onslaught. The last node. The most dangerous machine ever built.
Gatling spin attacks, phase teleportation, and relentless aggression.
“It was built to end wars. It will end YOU.”
